A tale that grows as fast as it started

When a union began and the rain clouds were parted

Two birds flying on two different paths

Happened to meet one October at last

Twas unexpected, but this caused neither to worry

The flight should be fun when they needn't not hurry

For they've get a friend in which they can confide

And someone to lean on right by their side

As the birds find the right tree to perch their nest in

The brave pair are left with just one question

How did we get so lucky for fate to align

For me to be yours and you to be mine?
